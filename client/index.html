ABOUT US

Welcome to the Homeless Shelter Finder. Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use platform to locate shelters and essential resources for those in need in New York City. We believe everyone deserves a safe place, and this tool is designed to connect individuals with the support available in their community.

You can use this website to search for shelters based on location or services, view them on an interactive map, and get detailed information. We are continuously working to update our listings and improve accessibility.
